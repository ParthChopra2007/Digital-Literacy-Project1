# Stay Safe Online – Checklist
## Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix letters, numbers, and symbols)
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, banking, and social media
Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
Be cautious of unknown links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS (they may be phishing scams)
Keep your social media accounts private and avoid oversharing personal details
Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store
## UPI Safety
Always verify the UPI ID or QR code before sending money
Never approve a “collect request” unless you are sure who sent it
Do not scan random QR codes to receive money
## Additional Safety
Regularly update your phone and apps to avoid security vulnerabilities
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments
If You Face Cyber Fraud or Crime
Report online at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call Helpline: 1930
